**Free. Open. No login. No tracking. For homeowners.**

A structured framework for homeowners to independently organize, verify, and analyze mortgage servicing records, property records, tax bills, insurance documents, and credit reports.

## What It DOES Do

✅ Helps you organize documents  
✅ Identifies inconsistencies between records  
✅ Flags missing information and gaps  
✅ Creates clear, neutral timelines  
✅ Generates questions to ask servicers  
✅ Produces regulator-ready summaries  
✅ Runs entirely in your browser  

## For Homeowners

**You need:**
- Your mortgage statements
- Payment records
- Escrow statements
- Tax bills
- Insurance documents
- Loan documents
- Credit reports (optional)
- 30 minutes to 2 hours

**You get:**
- Clear organization of all documents
- Documented discrepancies with dates and amounts
- Timeline of events
- List of missing records
- Questions to ask your servicer or attorney
- PDF summary ready to share
